# Contoso Support Policy

Contoso offers three support tiers for customers using the analytics platform.

The Standard tier includes email support with a target response time of two
business days. The Premium tier includes email and chat support with a target
response time of four business hours. The Enterprise tier includes a named
technical account manager, quarterly architecture reviews, and a one-hour target
response time for severity-one incidents.

Severity-one incidents are production outages that prevent most users from
accessing the platform. Customers should include the tenant ID, approximate
start time, recent deployment changes, and screenshots or logs when opening a
severity-one incident.
